    Day 1: Port Blair: Corbyn’s Cove Beach, Cellular Jail - Light And Sound Show

    Upon arrival at Port Blair, our team member can receive you and assist in boarding a cab to the appointed hotel. arrival and clean up whereafter our team will take you to Corbyn’s Cove Beach some 20 minutes’ drive.

    Corbyn’s Cove beach is celebrated for the exotic view of the coconut and palm-fringed beachline and turquoise blue sea, a perfect place for sunbathing and water sports activities from jet sport to boating, surfriding and swimming. it's an honest place to relax when a protracted flight.

    Later in the evening, we travel Cellular jail (KALA PANI) the colonial-era jail a standing landmark witness to the horrors and atrocities dispensed to freedom fighters. The attractive light-weight and Sound show at this historic site throws light on however freedom fighters fought or diminished into history.

    after the jail tour, our cabs take you to your resort. nightlong at Port Blair.

    02

    Day 2: (Rangat)Port Blair –Baratang - Rangat

    Before the day breaks, we will make progress to Baratang Island, which is around 110 km from Port Blair. Driving through the Andaman trunk road you will stop briefly at the Jikratng police check post and proceed further in convoys through the Jarawa Reserve forest said to be home to the Jarawa tribe. We will ride through the diverse tropical rainforest region, where you can enjoy the beauty and biodiversity of nature. After passing through the tribal reserve you would reach Nilambur Jetty. You would then be transferred to a vehicle ferry, which would take you to Baratang Island. These ferries can accommodate buses, trucks, cars & bikes, and passengers too, to cross the waters between Nilambur Jetty at Middle Strait and Baratang.

    Upon reaching Baratang board a smaller boat that will take you to the limestone cave site a 25 minutes boat ride through the unique mangrove trees and creeks on both sides creating a natural passage.

    The naturally formed limestone caves at Nayadera and the vast mangrove-covered creeks are major attractions for tourists in Baratang. A few moments later get back into your boats back to Baratang. Board our cabs and proceed to Rangat for lunch. On the way, we will stop at Amkunj Beach for some time before we move to our resort for check-in. Overnight stay at Rangat.

    03

    Day 3: (Diglipur)Rangat - Diglipur

    After breaskast and checkout board our cabs.First we visit Dhani Nallah beach in the morning today.Walk through Mangrove creek to reach this secluded Beach.Enjoy for couple of hours,then we proceed further towards Diglipur.In the afternoon,we stop at Mayabunder and head to Kramatang beach for couple of hours.

    In the evening,we leave for our final destination for the day, Diglipur check in to your resort.Overnight at Diglipur.

    04

    Day 4: (Diglipur)Ross &Smith Island

    After breakfast board our cabs and head to Aerial bay jetty,some 15 minutes drive.The day is dedicated to Rosss and Smith Island.Here a small boat will take you to Ross & Smith Island.

    Ross & Smith islands are actually two islands – Ross & Smith joined together by a sand bar to make a single group of islands. What makes these islands truly beautiful and sets them apart from the rest of the islands is that these two islands are joined by a natural,sand bar.With sea on both sides.Water is crystal clear and gem green in color.Sun bathing,Swimming and Snorkling in Ross & Smith Islands is a wonderful experience.You will see a lot of beautiful fish underwater.During high tide,the sand bar disappears leaving a trail of water between the two islands and during low tide the sand bar appears again making the islands look majestic! There is a Marine Sanctuary on the island which makes it an ideal place for spotting beautiful coral reefs and colourful species from the underwater.

    Enjoy your day at this magical destination.In the afternoon/evening board the boat to head back to Aerial Bay Jetty.Once back our cabs will take you back to your resort.Relax at your resort. Spend some time at Kalipur beach in the evening,which is at a 5 minutes walk from your resort.Overnight stay at Diglipur.

    05

    Day 5: (Diglipur)Mud Volcano and Ram Nagar Beach

    After breaskast,board our cabs and head to the famous Mud Volcanos of North Andaman. Located near Hathilevel at a distance of 20 kms from Diglipur a chain of Mud volcanoes can be witnessed in the green jungle of Shyamnagar at Diglipur a brisk 15 minutes walk in the semi-evergreen forests.First we find a big and dormant mud volcano but a little distance further oozing mud volcanos are seen.Seating facilities along with information boards are provided for visitors.

    After completion of Mud Volcano tour,we head to Diglipur.After lunch board our cabs and head to the Famous Ram Nagar Beach,for the rest of the eveing.It is a bit less popular beach as it is at a remote location.But it is an amazing beach with thick tropical forest reaching out to the shores.One can see the highest mountain peak of Andaman and Nicobar island called the Saddle Peak,upclose from here.Overnight stay at Diglipur/Rangat.

    06

    Day 6: (Port blair)Diglipur-Mayabunder-Rangat - Port Blair

    Today we start early after breakfast and head back to Port blair a 6-hour drive,about 300 km.In the afternoon,check in to your hotel. Evening free to rest or explore on your own.Overnight at Port blair.

    07

    Day 7: (Havelock)Port Blair - Havelock - Radhanagar Beach

    After breakfast and checkout ,our team member will take you to Haddo jetty by Cab,board the inter-island cruise to Havelock Island (57 kms by sea from Port Blair).

    Havelock is a picturesque natural paradise with beautiful white sandy beaches, rich coral reefs and lush green forest. It is one of the populated islands in the Andaman group of Island,with an area of 113 sq. km.On Arrival at Havelock Jetty, our team will assist you to board the AC Cab waiting to transfer you to the hotel.

    After lunch, board our Cabs and visit the World Famous “Radha Nagar Beach” situated on the western coast of Havelock Island,it is one of the most popular destinations for tourists in Andaman Islands.With its breathtaking sunsets,this beach is nothing less but a tropical heaven.Pristine sand,and the clear emerald blue waves and a lot of peace and serenity are the hallmark of this beautiful paradise.Witness the famous Sunset from Radhanagar beach and head back to your resort .Overnight at Havelock.

    08

    Day 8: (Havelock)Kalapathar beach & Elephanta Beach

    After breakfast,we head to Elephanta Beach, the ancient secluded beach.Elephanta beach is famous for all kind of water sports.Rest of the day is dedicated to your love of the ocean and associated water sports like scuba ,snorkeling,para sailing etc.

    Get charmed by the sugary white sand and the contrasting shadows of the old trees at the beach, which creates an illusionistic picturesque to mesmerise you. Spend the day snorkelling in the water and take the exotic view of the corals and the marine life under the sea, at Elephanta beach.

    In the evening,we proceed to Kala Pathar Beach.The beach gets its name from the surrounding village,and the black rocks that adorn the coastline here.Silvery sand and aqua green waters make the place exotic and exquisite and make this beach a great place to relax.It is a long stretch of white silky sand with big black rocks beside the blue sea and it is situated on one corner of Havelock Island.

    In the evening we return to our resort. Overnight stay at Havelock.

    09

    Day 9: (Port blair)Havelock – Port Blair

    After breakfast check out from hotel.Board your cabs ,our team will take you to Havelock jetty,board the cruise and return to Port blair.Upon Arrival,check in to your hotel at Port blair,relax for a while.In the evening our team will take you on a city tour,you can visit local Markets like Aderdeen Bazar,museums like samudrika emporium for shopping etc.,or visit the famous Flag Point .Overnight stay at Port Blair.

    10

    Day 10: (Port blair)Departure

    After breakfast and checkout,our cab will be waiting outside to take you to the Airport .
